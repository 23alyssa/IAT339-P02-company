Mother Daughter Reading Week

March 10th - 17th
Check Out Event

Bond with your daughter in our mother daughter reading week event. Read the books from our curated list or choose your own. Each book you and your daughter read together will enter you to win one of our draw prizes at the end of the week, the more books you read the more enteries you get! Our draw prizes will include; a 50$ gift card to The Book Worm, One free book of your choice, and a calligraphy set

Book Club

March 5th, 12th, 19th, 26th
Check Out Event

Come join our book club! we hold meetings every friday on Zoom to meet and discuss that weeks book. Be sure to find something you like because there is a different club for every genre. We have mystery, romance, horror, thriller, young adult, action/adventure, and fantasy. By looking on the list to see what book is currently being read by each club you are able to join whenever you want. Come have fun and chat books with us!

Check Out Event
