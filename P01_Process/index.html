Branding Elements

  1. What does the company do, or offer?
    My company is a not for profit organization that plants trees from donations. The Website would function as the portal for collecting donations from the users, as well as providing information on how many trees they have planted and which areas they have planted them. The main goal of this company is to combat deforestation around the world.
  2. Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)
    This company is a way for customers to make a difference in the environment, each amount donated will correlate to a certain number of trees being planted that the user will be able to feel good about.
  3. Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service
    Additionally to donating to plant trees, with donations of 10 dollars or more the user will receive a bracelet made from 100% recycled materials.
